China Beach

China Beach


Ratings

China Beach is a small cove in San Francisco's Sea Cliff neighborhood. It lies between Baker Beach and Lands End and is part of the Golden Gate National Recreation Area. This beach is pretty unknown to most tourists, which makes it perfect for locals to spend the day and relax with beautiful surroundings.

Location

China Beach, San Francisco, CA 94121

The Wave Organ

The Wave Organ

Ratings



A beautiful acoustic scultpture located on near the iconic Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco. It even contains more than 20 music pipes which extend into the bay. If you visit this hidden gem prepare to hear some harmonious liquid melodies. This also makes for a great 360 degree view of the city: Golden Gate Bridge on the left, Angel Island and Alcatraz ahead, FiDi on the right and the marina behind.




Location

83 Marina Green Dr, San Francisco, CA 94123

Clarion Alley

Clarion Alley

Ratings



Clarion Alley is located in a small street in San Francisco notable for the murals painted by the Clarion Alley Mural Project. It's simply street art at it's best. Murals with several different artistic styles that address cultural and political issues line either side of Clarion. If you're especially lucky, you might even visit when some of the artists are there and you can watch them in action or talk to them about their work.


Location

Clarion Alley, San Francisco, CA 94110

Bolinas Ridge

Ratings



Although it's one of the lesser known gems of San Francicso, the glorifying beauty of this is breathtaking.The trail is well marked, and offers spectacular views along the way.
