Mount Soledad

Mount Soledad


Ratings



The Veterans Memorial is one of the finest memorials ever built. Offering a breathtaking view of San Diego including the beach, city skyline, and beach houses. To add a personal element, the memorial contains war veterans encrusted on the walls. This unique memorial is one of the beautiful wonders of San Diego that goes overlooked. To enjoy a breathtaking scenery, travel to Mount Soledads to experience a true wonder.

Location

6905 La Jolla Scenic Dr S, La Jolla, CA 92037

Sunny Jim's Cave

Sunny Jim's Cave

Ratings







Inside a small wood-shingled building on La Jolla’s oceanfront Coast Blvd lies a beach-themed gift shop and access to one of La Jolla’s coolest attractions. The Sunny Jim Cave Store has the only entrance to any sea cave in California that you can access via land. The caverns within the cave are rich with beautiful colorings from earthly mineral deposits. Come take a voyage with us to this very popular place to visit and unique one of a kind place located right in the heart of La Jolla Village.








Location

1325 Coast Blvd, La Jolla, CA 92037
