Baked and Wired

Baked and Wired


Ratings



Baked and Wired is an artsy neighborhood bakery & coffeehouse supplying espresso drinks, big cupcakes & signature granola. It is overshadowed by the local Georgetown Cupcakes, but is cheaper, quicker and more delicious, giving the locals the real cupcakes they want.


Location

1052 Thomas Jefferson St NW, Washington, DC 20007

Kramerbooks and Afterwords

Kramerbooks and Afterwords


Ratings


Kramerbooks & Afterwords first opened its doors in 1976. At the time of its opening it was the first bookstore/cafe in Washington DC. Over the years, through expansions, subpoenas, and innumerable neighborhood changes, much of our business has remained constant. They provide their patrons with access to the best books, and to the most delicious, varied food. In addition to possessing a lively, convivial atmosphere, and a full-service bar, Kramerbooks & Afterwords stages hundreds of book-related events each year, both in the store and elsewhere. From tourists to neighbors, college students to the political elite, there is something for everyone at Kramerbooks & Afterwords!

Location

1517 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036

Renwick Gallery

Renwick Gallery


Ratings



The Renwick Gallery is home to the Smithsonian American Art Museum’s collection of contemporary craft and decorative art, one of the finest and most extensive collections of its kind. Compared to the other Smithsonian Muesams in the D.C., the Renwick Gallery is unique, quirky, and more hands-on. Although it is more "touristy" than other locations on the ExploR list, it is worth the small crowds due to the ever-changing galleries.

Location

1661 Pennsylvania Ave NW, Washington, DC 20006

Tropicalia Lounge

Tropicalia Lounge


Ratings



This lounge is a funky, colorful cocktail lounge hosting live performances from Brazilian, Latin & Caribbean acts. From a crew who've worked in DC nightlife for two decades, this underground, international "dance club for grownups" is tucked into a 14th & U corner, features bands & DJs reverberating off three huge, surrealist art installations, and a glass mosaic bar. This is the perfect place for over-21 locals to hang out and listen to new music.


Location

2001 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009



Howard Theatre

Howard Theatre


Ratings



This is a historic theater, that has been added to the National Register of Historic Places. It has hosted a diverse lineup of well-known acts, including Vic Mensa, Anthony Hamilton, Raheem DeVaughn, Chrisette Michele, Tamia, Dianne Reeves, Gregory Porter, Esperanza Spalding, José James, The Roots, Slick Rick, Drake, Chaka Khan, Aaron Neville, Kendrick Lamar, and Sheila E. This is the perfect place for locals to go see shows without huge crowds.

Location

620 T St NW, Washington, DC 20001
