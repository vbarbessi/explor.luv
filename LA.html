Behind the Hollywood Sign

Behind the Hollywood Sign


Ratings





The Hollywood sign is a large tourist attraction in LA, however, many people only want to get to the front, only about half way up the path. If you continue the trail, you can get behind the sign, which gives a wonderful view of the whole city. This is worth the walk, and may not be as crowded on a cool day.

Chandelier Tree

Chandelier Tree

Ratings

The Chandelier Tree is such a beautiful stop to make if you're in Silverlake or if you want to drive out to just see it. It is such a pretty thing to see especially as the evening got darker and the lights got brighter. This is on the corner of a house, and the homeowner is actually the one who created it, and luckily they allow people to stand around taking pictures. They take donations, so locals should definitely donate a couple coins to keep it up!

Location

2811 W Silverlake Dr Los Angeles, CA 90039
